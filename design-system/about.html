About Indica

A field guide to a nation, in numbers.

Indica answers questions about India with charts drawn from open data — and explains each one in three registers, from analyst to plain-spoken. We don't sell a point of view. We place every number beside its source and its companions, and let you draw the line.

How we work

01

Sourced

Every figure traces to a named, open archive and can be reproduced.

02

Numbers stay numbers

Explanations are AI-written prose around locked figures. The numbers are never generated.

03

Three registers

The Reading, In Plain English, and On the Ground — pick how you want it explained.

04

Many languages

Built multilingual from day one — explanations written natively, numbers formatted locally.

On the AI text

Explanatory writing is generated by a language model from the underlying data and cited sources, then reviewed. It may simplify. The "On the Ground" register is written in a warm, plain voice — and reviewed by native-language editors before it ships. When in doubt, read the methodology and the source.
